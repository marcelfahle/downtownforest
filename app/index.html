About Us

Downtown Forest

Hostel and Camping

Hostel, Camping and summer Grill rental Terrace. This is not a standard spiritless hotel. Here we try hard every day to make this hostel and camping a place where we would like to stay ourselves – slightly rustic and cozy. Our mission is tattooed on our strong arms: “provide a budget yet amazing experience of Vilnius”.

We call this place a forest, because green is all around our chateau, as we call it. The location is very downtown indeed: being located in the artistic part of Vilnius center called Užupis we have everything within a short-walk distance: City hall (12 min), Cathedral (18 min), bus & train station (23 min). Park (10 min), river and ponds (3 min) just nearby.

Our staff are all passionate about traveling and about our city. We treat our guests just what we would like to be treated when we are traveling.

Bar & Grill Rental

Bar

Our terrace becomes BAR & GRILL RENTING place on summer. It is perfect spot for cozy and atmospheric evening time. On winter you will find fireplace and good vibes inside the building.

Morning treasure

We serve fresh croissants every morning. Made by best-in-town pâtissier, our oven-baked, 1.20€/pcs. Book it in the evening. Every morning we serve tea & coffee for free.

Campsite

Campsite with all the facilities for your best stay.

GrillCamp

Our grill, your food. Make a picnic in the city center. Bring your food, meat, vegetables, friends and come to us, where you will find a lovely terrace for grilling and a bar with drinks. For a fixed price (min 12 eur per grill) we will give you grill, coal, and all the grilling tools.

For more information call +370 620 61529; info@downtownforest.lt

Camping

Our camping is the only camping in the Vilnius city center. We have 5 spots for campers and 15 tent places. We are open all year round and offer all amenities including:

  • Hot/cold water, Electricity, Guest kitchen
  • Free WIFI in your tent/camper
  • Bicycle rental
  • Bar & Grill rental place
  • 24 hour working reception
  • Free tea and coffee in the morning

Call us for prices and bookings: +370 686 84523
